Your Guide To Using The Aurelax Patches

How to Use

Step 1: Prepare the Skin

Spray water on a pulse area (e.g., behind ears, neck).

Step 2: Peel the Patch

Peel off the patch backing carefully.

Step 3: Apply the Patch

Firmly apply the patch to the wet skin.

Step 4: Secure the Patch

Press firmly for 5 seconds to ensure it sticks properly.
